Our Vision

A learning environment where students show how they think, educators trust what they see, and evidence of understanding guides growth. We envision classrooms where assessment enhances learning rather than interrupting it, where every student's unique thinking process is valued and understood.

Our Mission

To build trust and transparency in AI-mediated learning by making students' reasoning visible, supporting learner agency, and enabling educators to make informed decisions using authentic evidence of understanding. We're committed to creating assessment tools that celebrate thinking over memorization.
